    <p class="meta">
      <strong>Audience:</strong> Player, Clinician<br>
      <strong>Location:</strong> Canada / Global
    </p>

    <p>
      * Hosts a library of open-source assistive technology. Including devices for gaming such as assistive switches, joysticks, controller mods, switch interfaces, etc. Focus on low-cost solutions.
      * Focus in Canada, however resources and designs for the assistive technology are publically available for free.
      * Resources (this site and more) for adaptive gaming focused on both clinicians, organizations, and players.
      * The [GAME Checkpoints program](https://www.makersmakingchange.com/game-checkpoint-program) supports organizations, rehab hospitals, and community centres to obtain adaptive gaming gear and provide training for the equipment.
      * Allow player, families, or clinicians to submit a [GAME Ticket](https://www.makersmakingchange.com/create-a-gaming-ticket) that connects you with our adaptive gaming team for personalized support along your adaptive gaming journey.
    </p>

  <div class="support-content">
    <h3>[SpecialEffect](https://www.specialeffect.org.uk/)</h3>

    <p class="meta">
      <strong>Audience:</strong> Player<br>
      <strong>Location:</strong> United Kingdom / Global
    </p>

    <p>
      * Provides 1:1 support for individuals with physical disabilities to find the right gaming setup using a range of assistive technologies.
      * Known for expertise in eye-gaze systems, custom controller setups, and environmental control integration.
      * Conducts in-home or remote assessments to trial and configure gaming equipment.
      * Shares publicly available resources and case studies that demonstrate real-world adaptive gaming solutions.
      * Strong focus on improving quality of life through gaming, communication, and independence.
    </p>
  </div>
</div>
